Information We Collect ### 2.1 Personal Information We collect information that you provide directly to us: - **Account Information**: Email address, display name, password (encrypted) - **Profile Information**: Name, date of birth, expected due date - **Health Data**: - Pregnancy-related health metrics - Appointment schedules and reminders - Medication and supplement tracking - Weight - Notes and journal entries ### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information - **Device Information**: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers - **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent in app, crash reports - **Location Data**: Only if you explicitly grant permission for location-based features ### 2.3 Information from Third Parties - **Authentication Services**: If you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) - **Health Integrations**: Data from connected health apps (only with your explicit permission) ## 3. How We Use Your Information We use your information for the following purposes: ### 3.1 Core Functionality - Provide and maintain the app's features - Create and manage your account - Send appointment reminders and notifications - Track your pregnancy journey and health metrics - Generate personalized insights and recommendations ### 3.2 Communication - Send important updates about the app - Respond to your questions and support requests - Send optional educational content (with your consent) ### 3.3 Improvement and Analytics - Analyze app usage to improve features - Fix bugs and technical issues - Develop new features based on user needs ### 3.4 Legal Compliance - Comply with legal obligations - Protect against fraud and abuse - Enforce our terms of service ## 4. Data Storage and Security ### 4.1 Storage Location - **Local Storage (Default)**: ALL your data is stored locally on your device by default, encrypted with AES-256 - **Cloud Backup (Optional)**: If you enable cloud backup, data is encrypted and stored in YOUR OWN Google Drive account - **We Do NOT Store Your Data**: We do NOT have our own servers storing your health data - **No Data Upload by Default**: We do NOT upload your data to any server unless you explicitly enable cloud backup - **Your Google Account**: When you use cloud backup, the data goes directly to YOUR Google Drive, not our servers ### 4.2 Security Measures We implement industry-standard security measures: - **Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256) - **Authentication**: Secure password hashing using industry-standard algorithms - **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limit who can access your data - **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing - **No Sale of Data**: We never sell your personal health information ### 4.3 Data Retention - **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active - **Account Deletion**: All data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request - **Backup Data**: Backup copies deleted within 90 days - **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer if required by law ## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure ### 5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data We never sell, rent, or trade your personal health information to third parties. ### 5.2 Limited Sharing We may share your information only in these specific circumstances: - **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., exporting data to your healthcare provider) - **Google Services** (Only if you enable cloud backup): - Google Sign-In for authentication (your email and profile) - Google Drive for backup storage (data goes to YOUR account, not ours) - We do NOT have access to your Google Drive backups - Google's privacy policies apply to data stored in your Google account - **Analytics** (Optional, anonymized only): - Firebase Analytics for app usage statistics (NO personal health data shared) - Device information and app interactions only - You can opt-out in Settings - **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government regulation - **Emergency Situations**: To protect health and safety in emergencies - **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with advance notice) ### 5.3 Anonymized Data We may use and share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you personally for: - Research purposes - Public health initiatives - Product improvement ## 6.
